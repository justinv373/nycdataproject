What Is This Website About?

Since 2006, several high-profile shooting incidents occurred across Brooklyn, Queens, and Manhattan, contributing to concerns about public safety in New York City. One of the most notable was a random drive-by shooting spree in Queens in August 2006, during which a gunman killed one person and injured several others before being arrested. The incidents prompted major police investigations and renewed discussions about gun violence and crime prevention throughout the city.

Why Is This Website Important?

Creating a website about shootings since 2006 in New York City can help organize historical information in a clear and accessible way for students, researchers, and the public. Using code to build the site allows interactive features such as maps, and data visualizations that make the information easier to understand. This project can also help viewers view and learn about important historical events.
